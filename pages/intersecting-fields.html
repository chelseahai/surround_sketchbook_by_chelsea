Intersecting Fields

Examining the convergence of multiple disciplines and methodologies

The Convergence

This project brings together three worlds: computational design, fashion technology, and urban environmental data. It looks at how clothes can respond to real surrounding conditions, like weather, movement, and city systems, using algorithms. The goal is to imagine garments that aren't just worn, but lives in between our surrounds and us.
