Research Questions

Exploring fundamental inquiries that drive our research and design process

01
How can we design technology that responds to and enhances human creativity?
This question explores the intersection of human creativity and computational systems, examining how technology can be designed to augment rather than replace human creative processes.
02
What role does context play in the effectiveness of design interventions?
Investigating how environmental, social, and cultural contexts influence the success and adoption of design solutions in different settings.
03
How can we measure and evaluate the impact of design on human experience?
Developing frameworks and methodologies for assessing the qualitative and quantitative effects of design interventions on user experience and behavior.
04
What are the ethical implications of AI-driven design systems?
Examining the moral and social consequences of automated design processes, including issues of bias, transparency, and human agency.
05
How can design bridge the gap between technological innovation and human needs?
Exploring strategies for ensuring that technological advances are accessible, meaningful, and beneficial to diverse human populations.
